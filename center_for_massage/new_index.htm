Center for Massage & Natural Health

Massage School & Education Center

Our wonderful massage school, is located in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains of Asheville, NC. One of the most beautiful campuses in the country, and perhaps the world, this 6-month, 600-hour massage school offers a full and exciting curriculum, and is taught by highly trained and talented instructors from all over the state. We offer week-day and evening programs to allow for those who need to continue their current jobs while in school. This is one of the most sought-after massage schools in the region. Early enrollment is encouraged to reserve your seat. You will have the opportunity to graduate in only 6-months and begin a new exciting and fulfilling career in the healing arts and sciences. We are fully N.C. State Board Approved, NCBTMB Approved, an AMTA School Member and are in the application process for COMTA Accreditation. We hope to be fully accredited by fall 2006.

In addition, we offer Continuing Education programs, an Introduction to Massage workshop, and student housing. Read student testimonials and explore our area resources, alumni resources, a photo gallery, employment opportunities and more.

Natural Health Retreat Center

Visit our exclusive Natural Health Retreat Center in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Asheville, North Carolina. We offer unique vacation options for the discerning traveler, including overnight accommodations, vacation packages, health-oriented retreats, juice fast retreats, all-inclusives and much more. Our grounds are private, sprawling and spectacular, and include the beauty of a private lake, softly lit at night for romantic and peaceful strolling.

Massage Therapy Center

Specializing in our popular Student Massage Clinic, our Therapy Center is located in the heart of Asheville, North Carolina, a city now considered to be the country’s new “Healing Mecca,” among all the most popular restaurants and shops. Our student practitioners offer delightful and nurturing 50-minute treatment sessions for only $25 in a professional and soothing setting. Our wonderful professional massage therapists provide wonderful 60-120 minute sessions on our heated, plush massage tables, melting your stress away.
