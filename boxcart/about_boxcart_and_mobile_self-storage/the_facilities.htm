About BoxCart and Mobile Self-Storage

The facilities

BoxCart is located at 14790 Flint Lee Road, Chantilly, VA, 20151. MAP

Our 30,500 sq. ft. facility was designed and built around Security, Fire-protection, and Climate-control.

Security

We at BoxCart protect your belongings as if they were our own. Our facility is equipped with the latest, state-of-the-art electronic security equipment. We have created a security grid that covers every square foot of our facility. Through the use of motion sensors and beams, our facility is 100% electronically surveilled and centrally monitored.

Fire Protection

When your goods are stored with BoxCart, you can rest assured that they are not only constantly monitored for security, but also for fire. Our facility has every square foot covered by an automatic water-sprinkler system that is centrally monitored.

“Climate-control”…do you really know what that is?

If you have ever shopped around for storage, you'll know the term “climate-control” is thrown around with no regard for what it actually means or why you should insist on having it.

At BoxCart that’s just not good enough!!!

We know that excessive heat and cold, coupled with the DC-area humidity we are all familiar with, leads to mold and mildew. If you are like us, you don’t want those things creeping into your precious goods. Therefore, our one-of-a-kind facility is equipped with air-conditioning, heat, and most importantly, humidity control. Your goods will be kept at a relative humidity of 50%-60% in a temperature-controlled environment to protect against and minimize the harmful effects of high humidity. You will not find another facility with this level of protection in the storage business!!
