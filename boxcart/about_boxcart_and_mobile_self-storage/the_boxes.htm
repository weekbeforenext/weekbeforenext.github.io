About BoxCart and Mobile Self-Storage

The BoxCarts

BoxCarts are un-paralleled in the industry, designed with your needs in mind. Here are just a few specifications to note:

  • Compact enough to fit in a parking spot
  • Economical enough to use multiple containers to separate heavier
    and bulkier furniture and appliances from lighter and more fragile goods
  • Weatherproof so they can be stored outside
  • Ventilated to ensure appropriate air flow

We have 2 types of BoxCarts - our Air-conditioned Facility Storage BoxCart and our On-Site Storage BoxCart. Both have the following specifications:

  • 5’ wide x 8’ deep x 7 ½ ‘ tall
  • Square feet: 40
  • Cubic feet: 300
  • Weather-proof
  • Double locking system
  • Hold approximately 1 to 1 ½ rooms
  • Designed to accomodate items such as armoires, sofas, washers, dryers, and other large or heavy items

So what’s the difference? Check it out below.

Air-Conditioned Facility Storage BoxCart

Designed for:

Short-term outside storage while loading goods, then to be stored in our secure, air-conditioned, humidity controlled facility.

 

Uses:

  • Decluttering before a sale
  • In-town moves
  • Students' stuff during breaks
  • Military relocations and tours of duty

On-Site Storage BoxCart

Designed for:

Long-term outside storage. Welded-hinge construction provides secure on-site storage.

 

Uses:

  • Remodeling
  • Renovations
