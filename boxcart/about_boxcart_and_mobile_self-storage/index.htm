About BoxCart and Mobile Self-Storage

The Company

Mobile self-storage is the “drive-through” of personal self-storage, and BoxCart green™ is to mobile self-storage as brown is to shipping. We provide all the benefits of traditional self-storage, but we make it more convenient and more hassle-free than ever. It’s the “in” storage concept catching on across the country, and BoxCart is now making this portable storage service available in the greater DC Metro area.

BoxCart delivers secure weatherproof steel storage containers directly to your door. You pack them in a day, a week – whatever works with your schedule – then call us for pick-up. We’ll take your BoxCarts to our air-conditioned, humidity-controlled storage facility or to your new local destination. We even offer discounted packing supplies. Nothing could be easier!

Want to see how it works? View our TV ad!! Just click the button on the left.

When you choose to store your goods with BoxCart, you can visit our facility to access your belongings. Just give us a call a few hours ahead, so we have time to move your BoxCarts to our private customer viewing area. If you prefer, we can also deliver your BoxCarts back to your location for an additional fee.

BoxCart is locally owned and operated out of Chantilly, VA. OUR MISSION is to make your storage and moving experience as easy and stress-free as possible. Our entire operation is based on customer-friendly polices that PUT YOU IN CONTROL. Simply put, our pledge is TOTAL CUSTOMER SATISFACTION!
