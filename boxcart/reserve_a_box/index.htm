Reserve a BoxCart

Click here to check availability and make an appointment!

There are two steps to Reserve Your BoxCarts:


Step 1: Check availability and make an appointment

Appointments are initially made in two categories:

  • Morning appointments:  9:00am to 1:00pm (choose 9am on calendar)
  • Afternoon appointments: 1:00pm to 5:00pm (choose 3pm on calendar)

** Your specific appointment time will be established with BoxCart delivery personnel no later than 48 hours prior to the date of service. **

If delivery or pickup is unavailable on the date or time you want, please call us at 703-263-3300 or toll free at 1-866-330-CART (2278), and we'll make every effort to accommodate your schedule.

Once you have made an appointment, you will automatically receive an e-mail confirmation. This may take a few moments based on your provider. If you encounter any problems, please call us at one of our above phone numbers.

To get started, Make an Appointment, or Call us at one of our above phone numbers and we’ll be glad to answer any of your questions and take care of everything for you.

Step 2: Finalize your BoxCart Reservation

After you have made your appointment, a BoxCart representative will call you to discuss your specific needs and assist you in determining which BoxCart service is most appropriate. We will also explain our billing and operational procedures and finally, set-up and activate your account and re-confirm your appointment.
