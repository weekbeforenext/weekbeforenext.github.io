Services and Pricing

As you may have noticed, we at BoxCart think simplicity is important when it comes to storage and moving, and it can’t get much simpler than our services and pricing. We have two options to serve your storage and moving needs -- On-Site Storage and BoxCart Facility Storage.

  • No minimum storage period required!
  • All rates are daily rates, and you're only charged for actual days used!
  • We only charge a ONE-TIME Delivery Fee of $95 + tax (includes Pick-up)
